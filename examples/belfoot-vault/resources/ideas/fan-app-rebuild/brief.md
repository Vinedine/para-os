# Fan app rebuild (idea)

> Concept-stage. No committed deliverable, deadline, or budget yet. Dependent on the [ticketing platform replacement](../../../projects/ticketing-platform-replacement/brief.md) landing first. Promote to `projects/` once a go/no-go is on the calendar or budget is committed.

Rebuild the BelFoot fan mobile app on top of the new ticketing API, folding the cashless wallet and matchday services into one app.

## Why now

- The current app is a thin wrapper over the 2014 ticketing platform; it dies with that platform.
- Once the new ticketing API and the cashless wallet exist, the app is the natural front door for both - tickets, top-ups, resale, and matchday info in one place.
- Commercial (see [Thomas Vermeulen](../../../areas/network/thomas-vermeulen.md)) has asked for in-app sponsor activation inventory.

## Possible scope

- Wallet top-up and balance in-app.
- Ticket purchase, transfer, and resale on the new secondary market.
- Matchday: gate, seat, concessions map, live queue times.
- Push notifications and sponsor activation surfaces.

## Open questions

- Build vs. buy: does the new ticketing vendor's white-label app cover enough?
- Native vs. cross-platform?
- Who owns it after launch - club marketing or IT?

## Dependencies

- Ticketing platform replacement (vendor API must be live).
- Cashless wallet (live for the 2026-27 season).
