# Statement of Work - NovaPay closed-loop cashless system

**Parties:** BelFoot Royal Sporting Club (client) and NovaPay NV, Antwerp (supplier)
**Signed:** 2026-04-18
**Reference:** NP-SOW-2026-114

## Scope of supply

NovaPay will design, supply, install, and commission a closed-loop cashless payment system for the BelFoot stadium, covering:

- Closed-loop wallet provisioned on the existing season-ticket NFC chip, plus a top-up app for non-members.
- 47 concession terminals and 12 merchandise terminals (NovaPay TERM-5 hardware).
- Stadium payment network: cabled LAN to each point of sale with managed 4G fallback.
- 8 self-service top-up kiosks (one per gate) and an online top-up route.
- Refund and dispute back office.

Out of scope: outside-the-stadium retail, the loyalty programme, and any change to the season-ticket CRM.
