# Request for Proposal - Ticketing platform replacement

**Issued by:** BelFoot Royal Sporting Club
**Issued:** 2026-05-15
**Responses due:** 2026-06-20, 17:00 CET
**Contact:** Sofie Vanhove (IT Director), via the engagement Drive
**Issued to:** Ticketmaster Sport, SeatGeek Enterprise, AXS, TicketMatch BE

## Background

BelFoot's ticketing platform has been in production since 2014 and reaches end of support in Q1 2027. We are selecting a modern, API-first replacement that supports dynamic pricing, a native secondary market, and clean integration with our Salesforce CRM and the new closed-loop cashless wallet.

## Mandatory requirements

1. Open REST/GraphQL API covering inventory, pricing, orders, and access control.
2. Native dynamic pricing with rule- and demand-based models.
3. Native secondary market (fan-to-fan resale with configurable price caps).
4. Bidirectional Salesforce CRM integration.
5. Migration of ~24,000 season-ticket-holder accounts with full history.
6. Phased cutover: corporate hospitality first, then general admission.
7. GDPR-compliant data residency in the EU.
8. SSO with the club's existing identity provider.

## Evaluation criteria

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Functional fit (mandatory + desirable) | 35% |
| Integration & API quality | 20% |
| Total cost of ownership over 5 years | 20% |
| Migration approach & risk | 15% |
| Vendor viability & reference clubs | 10% |
